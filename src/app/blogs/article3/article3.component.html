Are you Investing without a Financial Goal?

This is a short & crisp dialogue from a “Book Alice In Wonderland” -

“Would you tell me, please, which way I ought to go from here? “Alice asked the Cheshire cat.

“That depends a good deal on where you want to get to,” said the cat. “I don’t much care where- “said Alice. Then it doesn’t matter which way you go”, said the cat. “…so long as I get SOMEWHERE”, Alice added as an explanation. “OH, you are sure to do that, “said the Cat, “IF YOU ONLY WALK LONG ENOUGH” If you don’t know where you are going any road will take you there.

Financial Planning helps you to build your road maps towards your destination. The first step of financial planning is to DEFINE SMART GOALS. Else it doesn’t matter where you invest or how much you earn…
